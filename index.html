Hi! I'm Samantha, a graphic designer and digital artist!

Daydreams from Digital Paradise is a really special project to me. An interactive desktop, it is meant to show a glimpse into an artist's digital dream world. I designed this web experience using HTML, CSS, and jQuery. I was mentored by front-end web developer Vanessa Sickles. As someone who was relatively new to coding (and really wanted to learn more), I was extremely lucky to have such a kind and knowledgable teacher!

In my research, I sought to explore the concept of digital escapism - especially thinking about the millennial (and other generations to follow) experience of being raised in the age of the internet. As a digital artist, my computer is more than a medium to make art - it is a place where I can express myself through art and find endless inspiration. The internet has really shaped me as an artist and I wanted to make something that authentically told this story!

This website was made for Graphic Design Senior Studio at Wayne State University. See work by other students here.
